# Consent Form - Prolific

## Research Study Information

You are being invited to participate in a research study through Prolific. Please read this form carefully and ask any questions you may have before agreeing to participate.

### Purpose of the Study
This study aims to evaluate the effectiveness of AI-powered code analysis tools in assessing non-functional requirements (NFRs) in software systems.

### Procedures
If you agree to participate, you will:
1. Complete a tutorial on the system
2. Evaluate AI-generated assessments of NFRs
3. Provide feedback through surveys and forms
4. Complete a satisfaction survey
5. Provide your Prolific ID for compensation

### Duration
The study is expected to take approximately 60-90 minutes to complete.

### Compensation
You will be compensated according to Prolific's standard rates for studies of this duration.

### Risks and Benefits
There are no significant risks associated with this study. The benefits include contributing to research in software engineering and AI.

### Confidentiality
Your responses will be kept confidential and used only for research purposes. Your Prolific ID will be used solely for compensation purposes.

### Voluntary Participation
Your participation in this study is completely voluntary. You may withdraw at any time without penalty, though compensation may be affected if the study is not completed.

### Contact Information
If you have questions about this study, please contact the research team through Prolific's messaging system.
